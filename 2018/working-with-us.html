OUR
STORY

It's a short one.

Like so many logos, ours began sketched on a cocktail napkin.

The idea was to always be “On-Target!” by focusing on tangible business goals and strategies. Our simplified motto was, "Reach the right people, at the right time with the right message."

16 years later, Scott Steiner and Tasha Steiner are at the forefront of a successful, award-winning digital marketing and advertising agency in Houston, frequently found on the Houston Business Journal's ”Houston's Top Advertising Agencies” or “Fast 100 Fastest Growing Companies” or "Fast Tech 50" lists.

While the tactics have changed drastically over the years, the brand strategies, media plans, website developments, and overall communication techniques remain focused on our simplified motto, and the results continue to impress.
